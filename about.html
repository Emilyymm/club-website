About Our Club

The San Jac Esports Club is a community of passionate gamers, strategists, and team players. We aim to create an environment where students can compete, connect, and grow both in and out of the game. Whether you’re into shooters, MOBAs, or strategy games, there’s a place for you here.

Meet Our Administrator

David Thomas

David Thomas (a.k.a DJ) is the driving force behind San Jac Esports. With years of gaming experience and a passion for student engagement, DJ ensures that every member has the support they need to reach their potential both in tournaments and in teamwork.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do we compete against other colleges?

Yes! Our teams regularly participate in online scrimmages against other colleges each week, and we also travel to in-person tournaments hosted by different campuses throughout the season.

Where can I find updates and announcements?

Join our Discord! You’ll find all the latest updates, event info, and announcements there.
Join our Discord Server

Can I join more than one team?

Yes, you can join multiple teams as long as you’re able to commit to each team’s practice schedule and scrimmages.
